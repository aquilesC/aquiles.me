Many people will have an opinion on the [[product]] and its features, but not everyone will be a *stakeholder*. For a [[product manager]] it is important to understand the distinction, since stakeholders are the ones that can have veto power, and the ones who should ultimately be heard [@cagan2018]. 

Most likely stakeholders are: 

- The CEO and the leaders of marketing, sales, and technology
- Business parners
- Finance
- Legal
- Compliance
- Business development (to make sure there are no violations of contracts or relationships)
