<!-- START_ABSTRACT -->
Ferroelectric e-WO3 nanoparticles were synthesized and stabilized above room temperature for the first time. A sensor based on this material was found to be highly sensitive and selective to acetone gas which will serve as a good candidate for diabetes diagnosis based on human breath analysis.
