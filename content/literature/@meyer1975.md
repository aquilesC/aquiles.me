<!-- START_ABSTRACT -->
The authors have studied the effect of tool-making on man's evolution. They argue that growth is not exponential, but hyperbolic, and that a positive feedback exists linking technological development to population. Exploring the nature of this mechanism in order to improve the predictive capability of global models is the goal of the article.
