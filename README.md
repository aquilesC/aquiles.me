# Aquiles Carattino's Digital Garden

Welcome to the repository for my digital garden, published at [notes.aquiles.me](https://notes.aquiles.me).

## What is this?

This is a collection of my thoughts, notes, and ideas explicitly linked together to form a graph of knowledge. It is built around the concepts of the Zettelkasten method and evergreen notes. Unlike a traditional blog, there is no strict structure, chronological order, or expectation of polished articlesâ€”just a free flow of thoughts interconnected by backlinks. 

Through the relationship between notes taken far apart in time, mood, and epistemic status, I try to build new insights. This digital garden is a forever-growing *work in progress*, gardened in public.

## About Me

My name is Aquiles Carattino. I am a physicist, entrepreneur, and business developer. I'm passionate about microscopy, optics, nanoparticles, programming, and tackling challenges like climate change, open source, privacy, and digital education.
